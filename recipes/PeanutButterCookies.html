Peanut Butter Cookies

  • Steps
  • Go back

To make this Peanut Butter Cookies need this ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup crunchy peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Directions

  1. Step

    2. Cream Butter peanut and sugars together in a bowl; beat in eggs.

  2. Step

    3. In a separate bowl sift flour baking powder baking soda and salt; stir into butter mixture. Put dough in refrigerator for 1 hour.

  3. Step

    4. Roll dough into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a fork making a crisscross pattern. Bake in a preheated 375 degrees F oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown.
