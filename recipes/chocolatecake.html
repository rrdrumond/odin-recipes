To make this chocolate cake need this ingredients

  • 2 eggs size L
  • 280g white suggar
  • 120ml of oliva o
  • 160ml of milk
  • 1 tea spoon of vanila extract
  • 180g of wheat flour
  • 60g of cocoa
  • 2 tea spoon of baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 100ml hot watter

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs from 5 min
  2. add the suggar
  3. After 2 minutes of mix add the oliva oil
  4. Add the milk
  5. Add the vanila
  6. Add the wheat flour with the baking powder and mix slowly for 1 or 2 minutes
  7. Add the salt and cocoa and just mix for half minute and then add hot watter and mix half minute more
  8. Cook at 180 celcius degrees for 35 to 40 minutes
